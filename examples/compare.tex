with legs $x$ and $y$ and hypotenuse $z$.

This document exercises several LaTeX features that our compiler supports,
allowing us to compare the output of RustLaTex against pdflatex.

  \item Inline math mode renders superscripts and operators correctly.
  \item Itemize lists produce bulleted items with proper indentation.
\end{itemize}
